About Me

My love for puzzles started when I began doing the Monday crossword in the NYT weekly when i was 16; there is almost nothing more exciting than the moment the last puzzle piece is fit into its place. My love for problem solving and learning paired with my inquisitive personality led me to be a self taught e-commerce enthusiast, and now a software developer. Watching a project come together, with all the features seamlessly working in sync is one of my favorite parts of coding. I love working with Python and enjoy integrating Javascript and React.js into my work. When I'm not coding, I love to read anything and everything I can get my hands on, from fantasy to sci-fi to thrillers, and spend as much time as I can outdoors with family and friends. I play SWGOH religiously, and love Harry Potter almost as much as Star Wars.
